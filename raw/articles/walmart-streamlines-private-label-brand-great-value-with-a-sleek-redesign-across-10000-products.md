The bigger the brand, the bigger the redesign—and it doesn’t get much bigger than Walmart.  
品牌越大，改版规模就越大——而沃尔玛的规模堪称业界之最。

The behemoth retailer has just unveiled a massive overhaul of its flagship private label brand, Great Value, including the redesign of nearly 10,000 food and household items. That’s a lot of.ai files.  
这家零售巨头刚刚对其旗舰自有品牌 Great Value 进行了大规模改造，包括重新设计近万种食品和家居用品。这涉及大量.ai 文件。

“Great Value is already in 9 out of 10 U.S. homes, so with that kind of legacy, the team needed to ensure we were thoughtfully evolving the brand with this redesign,” says Maya McDonald, Senior Director of Always-On Consumer & Campaigns at Walmart. “Maintaining the consumer trust and credibility we’ve already built was critical throughout the process, while ensuring that the new design felt like a meaningful upgrade.”  
沃尔玛长期消费者与营销活动高级总监玛雅·麦克唐纳表示："全美 90%的家庭已在使用'超值'产品，面对如此深厚的品牌积淀，团队必须确保此次改版能实现品牌的精心演进。在重塑过程中，既要维系已建立的消费者信任与品牌信誉，又要让新设计呈现真正意义上的升级感。"

And a meaningful upgrade they’ve made, scrubbing Great Value’s sea of dated, disjointed designs and upgrading to a clear and cohesive visual language suited for the modern day shopper.  
他们确实实现了质的飞跃：彻底摒弃了"超值"品牌陈旧割裂的视觉体系，升级为清晰连贯的视觉语言，以契合当代消费者的审美需求。

![](https://i0.wp.com/thedieline.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/great-value-press-center-12.jpg?w=1920&quality=70&ssl=1)

![](https://i0.wp.com/thedieline.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/great-value-press-center-1.jpg?w=1920&quality=70&ssl=1)

The impetus for such a massive undertaking was closing the gap between the way Walmart customers perceive and regard the Great Value brand and how it showed up on the shelf. “Customers already trusted and embraced the products, but the packaging expression hadn’t kept up,” says McDonald. “The reality is that it had been 10 years since Great Value was last redesigned, and over time, the brand became fragmented as it evolved category by category, which made it less consistent and harder to shop. The redesign was about creating a more unified system that improves recognition and aligns the brand with our customers’ evolving expectations.”  
启动如此大规模改造的动因，在于弥合沃尔玛顾客对惠宜品牌的认知与货架实际呈现之间的差距。"顾客早已信任并喜爱这些产品，但包装设计未能与时俱进，"麦克唐纳解释道，"事实上，惠宜品牌已有十年未进行整体升级，随着品类逐步扩展，品牌形象逐渐碎片化，导致统一性不足且购物体验欠佳。本次重塑旨在建立更统一的视觉体系，提升品牌辨识度，使品牌形象与顾客不断升级的期待同步。"

![](https://i0.wp.com/thedieline.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/great-value-press-center-8.jpg?w=1920&quality=70&ssl=1)

The refreshed Great Value look, envisioned by Walmart’s in-house design team and JKR, was designed to meet the needs of the brand’s many, many customers in terms of form and function. The existing Great Value brand needed a cleaner, more modern visual appeal while increasing accessibility and shopability across both in-store and digital shopping experiences.  
由沃尔玛内部设计团队与 JKR 联合构思的新版惠宜视觉系统，致力于从形式与功能双重维度满足海量顾客的需求。原有品牌形象需要更简洁现代的视觉呈现，同时提升实体店与数字购物场景中的可识别性与选购便利性。

“At this scale, our focus was on building a system that could balance consistency with flexibility,” says McDonald. “The design prioritizes a clear hierarchy and consistent placement of information. It also takes a purposeful approach to communication—highlighting what’s useful and making it easy to find—while maintaining a cohesive brand that can still adapt across such a broad range of different categories.”  
"在这个规模上，我们的重点是建立一个既能保持一致性又能兼顾灵活性的系统，"麦克唐纳表示。"设计优先考虑清晰的信息层级和一致的布局。它采用了一种有目的的沟通方式——突出有用信息并使其易于查找——同时保持一个统一的品牌形象，能够适应如此广泛的不同品类。"

![](https://i0.wp.com/thedieline.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/great-value-press-center-4.jpg?w=1920&quality=70&ssl=1)

![](https://i0.wp.com/thedieline.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/great-value-press-center-2.jpg?w=1920&quality=70&ssl=1)

Achieving an elevated look also meant stripping away tired design elements like the drop shadow and oval lockup on the wordmark, and developing a sleeker graphic style to depict each product. To enhance Great Value’s brand equity and name recognition, each product now prominently features the refreshed Great Value logo, displayed in a large navy box across many items in the range.  
要实现更高端的外观，意味着需要摒弃陈旧的视觉元素，例如标志中的阴影和椭圆形锁扣，并采用更简洁的图形风格来呈现每款产品。为了提升 Great Value 的品牌价值和知名度，现在每款产品都醒目地展示了焕然一新的 Great Value 标志，该标志以深蓝色大框的形式出现在该系列的众多商品上。

Other improvements to the packaging include consistent placement of nutrition information and benefit claims across all Great Value food items. Visual cues are now more direct and streamlined for enhanced visibility and an easier shopping experience in identifying items in person and digitally.  
包装的其他改进包括在所有惠宜食品上统一放置营养信息和益处声明。视觉提示现在更加直接和简洁，以增强可见性，并提供更便捷的购物体验，无论是线下还是线上识别商品。

To ensure a smooth transition across so many products, the new look will be rolled out in phases over the next two years, starting with salty snacks and then expanding incrementally to other categories.  
为确保如此多产品的平稳过渡，新外观将在未来两年内分阶段推出，从咸味零食开始，然后逐步扩展到其他类别。

![](https://i0.wp.com/thedieline.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/great-value-press-center-10.jpg?w=1920&quality=70&ssl=1)

![](https://i0.wp.com/thedieline.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/great-value-press-center-6.jpg?w=1920&quality=70&ssl=1)

This redesign marks the most extensive private brand update in Walmart’s history. The quality of what they’ve created proves that budget-friendly brands don’t need to look like they’ve been designed *on* a budget. As Walmart’s Vice President of Creative, David Hartman, puts it, “We believe great design should be accessible to everyone.” At a time in the US when prices are skyrocketing, and purse strings are tightening, it’s especially important for brands like Great Value to up their design game as more and more people turn to their products and rely on savvy, thoughtful packaging. Walmart is setting a new bar for budget-conscious brands everywhere, and the caliber of design they can and should achieve.  
此次重新设计标志着沃尔玛历史上最广泛的自有品牌更新。他们所创造的产品质量证明，平价品牌无需看起来像是预算有限的设计。正如沃尔玛创意副总裁大卫·哈特曼所言：“我们相信优秀的设计应该人人可得。”在美国物价飞涨、消费紧缩的时期，随着越来越多的人转向并依赖精明周到的包装产品，像“超值”这样的品牌提升设计水平显得尤为重要。沃尔玛正在为全球注重预算的品牌以及它们能够且应该达到的设计水准设定新标杆。
