![](https://thedieline.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/Dieline_BREVRN-NASA_01.jpg.avif)

*BREVRN™ is a new premium home fragrance brand dedicated to innovation in both scent and design. Our NASA line of premium soy candles, blended and poured in the USA, showcases fragrances that reflect the incredible rate of technological and societal progress in the United States during the second half of the 20th century. Our packaging solution draws inspiration from NASA’s iconic modernist design principles of visual simplicity, practical functionality, and an unwavering focus on safety.  
*

![](https://thedieline.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/Dieline_BREVRN-NASA_04.jpg.avif)

*Primary Packaging: Robust stainless steel canisters were the natural choice for this line, providing an ideal vessel for housing candles of this type. Unlike glass and ceramic, stainless steel is lightweight, decoratively versatile, resistant to breakage during transit, and can withstand high heat during use. Its simple, single-material construct is recyclable at standard recycling facilities. The custom-fit steel lid extends the product’s shelf life by reducing fragrance oil evaporation and doubles as a reliable flame snuffer. For ease of scent navigation, we decorated the canisters with bold, branded colors and highly legible modernist type treatments, offering an aesthetic that honors retro NASA branding while remaining timeless.*

![](https://thedieline.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/Dieline_BREVRN-NASA_03.jpg.avif)

*  
To ensure compliance with ASTM Candle Fire Safety standards, we used a third-party testing lab to verify that our product and labeling meet these requirements. The label specifies: ”Stop use when only 1/4 inch of wax remains.” To aid customers, we incorporated a “Red Zone” of wax that changes from white to red as the candle nears the end, providing a clear visual cue that it’s time to discontinue use.*

![](https://thedieline.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/Dieline_BREVRN-NASA_05.jpg.avif)

![](https://thedieline.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/Dieline_BREVRN-NASA_02.jpg.avif)

  
*Secondary Packaging: Reflecting the high quality of ingredients and elevated price point, we aimed to create a high-end presentation box that avoids less sustainable materials like plastics and foams, commonly used in products of this nature. The solution is a fully corrugated E-Flute carton that cradles the candle securely with strategically placed air gaps for impact protection. The double-door construction not only provides added durability but also offers an anticipatory unboxing experience, allowing for storytelling and reinforcing safety information.*

*We also integrated a convenient ejection mechanism into the carton liner, making candle retrieval effortless while adding a NASA-inspired theatrical flair to the process. This packaging provides a responsible, sustainable solution that delivers an enjoyable and informative customer experience while offering high-level protection for both wholesale retail and direct-to-consumer settings.*
