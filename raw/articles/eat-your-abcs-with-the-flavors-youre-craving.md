*Many Americans are deficient in specific vitamins and minerals, and the latest lifestyle trends have us all looking for clean protein sources. ABC360 is an LA-based, on-the-go meal bar system that delivers 33% of your daily macros, vitamins, and minerals. It is the only meal bar on the market that uses micro-encapsulated technology to flavor mask and ensure maximum absorption of all 26 essential vitamins and minerals.*

![](https://i0.wp.com/thedieline.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/9-ABC-protein-meal-bar-packaging-Atlas-Branding-scaled.jpg?resize=2048%2C1654&quality=70&ssl=1)

![](https://i0.wp.com/thedieline.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/5-ABC-protein-meal-bar-packaging-Atlas-Branding.jpg?w=1800&quality=70&ssl=1)

*With these unique qualifiers, our studio worked with the idea of essentials and the fundamentals of ABC and incorporated primary color. The bars are designed for high contrast so consumers can quickly tell each apart to select which bar to eat during the day. The A bar contains ingredients to “Activate” gut health and start your day. The B bar’s ingredients “Boost” midday energy, while the C bar’s adaptogens “Calm” your system to ease daily stress.*

![](https://i0.wp.com/thedieline.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/3-ABC-protein-meal-bar-packaging-Atlas-Branding-scaled.jpg?resize=2048%2C1330&quality=70&ssl=1)

*Using contrasting colors and typographic patterns brings a modern restraint to a market saturated with ultra-realistic ingredient illustrations and copy-heavy packaging. Each daily meal bar comes in various flavors with a delineated area on the front for the essential details. It displays what looks like a button you can almost swipe, signaling the brain of the purpose and significance of each meal bar.*
