*Mittereum is an innovative company focused on the field of aromatherapy, whose main objective is to intertwine the threads of tradition and modernity, combining the pure essence of nature with the most cutting-edge technological advances.*

![](https://i0.wp.com/thedieline.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/mittereum_03.jpg?resize=2048%2C1152&quality=70&ssl=1)

![](https://i0.wp.com/thedieline.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/mittereum_01.jpg?resize=2048%2C1152&quality=70&ssl=1)

*They aimed to develop a complete range of products to establish themselves as a revolutionary reference in the world of aromatherapy, offering everything from personalized sets to candles and bath bombs. Our challenge was to create a comprehensive collection of products that aligned with MITTEREUM’s futuristic vision.*

*With these principles in mind, we developed a collection that evokes sophistication and luxury, based on the brand’s different aromas and distinguishing them through its product SKUs.*

![](https://i0.wp.com/thedieline.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/mittereum_04.jpg?resize=2048%2C1152&quality=70&ssl=1)

![](https://i0.wp.com/thedieline.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/mittereum_06.jpg?resize=2048%2C1152&quality=70&ssl=1)

*  
We selected the most iconic scents of the brand, and under the theme of unity, we created a futuristic concept, embossed with foil on the top in each aroma’s corresponding color.*

![](https://i0.wp.com/thedieline.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/mittereum_05.jpg?resize=2048%2C1152&quality=70&ssl=1)

*The brand’s core value is the fusion of tradition and modern technology. The visual system we created is designed to be scalable, allowing for seamless expansion with future products the brand may introduce.*

![](https://i0.wp.com/thedieline.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/mittereum_08.jpg?resize=2048%2C1152&quality=70&ssl=1)
