*The Background*

*Packed with collagen protein and coated in chocolate, BUILT has mastered a truly delicious flavor-forward range of “Puffs” and Bars for its fan base. They kickstarted operations as a D2C company but have grown to have a retail presence in Walmart, Sam’s Club and Costco.*

![](https://i0.wp.com/thedieline.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/250318_Built_Brownie_Old-1-scaled.png?resize=2048%2C1657&quality=65&ssl=1)

*They had an IFYKYK reputation, which created a cult following, but the brand and the packaging were lackluster, and to win at retail, a new strategy and packaging system was needed. Our task was to create a standout visual identity, with amplified taste-appeal and clear differentiation between its “Puff” and “Bar” range supported by its low sugar, high protein & better-for-you claims.*

![](https://i0.wp.com/thedieline.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/250318_Built_Coconut_New-1-scaled.png?resize=2048%2C1657&quality=65&ssl=1)

*The Work*

*We solidified BUILT’s strategic foundation and defined its persona to use as a springboard for design. We reimagined the wordmark and the packaging portfolio architecture, bringing in a more contemporary wordmark and amped up appetite appeal to deliver on the brands’ unique PUFF texture and delicious (borderline indulgent) taste.*
