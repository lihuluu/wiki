*The core visual auxiliary graphics of the product are constantly changing geometric figures, which change from small to large, and extend from weak to strong, which accurately conveys the efficacy and psychological hinting characteristics of the product.*

![Editorial photograph](https://i0.wp.com/thedieline.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/04/e4beae23-149a-4b01-877c-e04034db2e98__20211109104241.png?w=1920&quality=65&ssl=1)

Editorial photograph

*The creative point of inspiration for auxiliary graphics development originates from Op Art. The special arrangement of auxiliary lines, shapes and colors can cause people’s optical illusions, so that the static picture can produce a dazzling and flowing dynamic effect. According to this feature, we use it in On the packaging, it not only fits the concept conveyed by the packaged product, but also makes the packaging more interesting and youthful.*

![Editorial photograph](https://i0.wp.com/thedieline.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/04/0ae376df-1adf-41d1-a4a2-18cea918efd4__202111091042411.jpg?w=1920&quality=70&ssl=1)

Editorial photograph

*Silver-gray background color selection, youthful personality and unique consumption concept make the product more textured and more professional. We chose the most popular “Klein blue” as the main visual color for the auxiliary packaging graphics this summer. The pure and strong characteristics of Klein blue coincide with Mr. Hall’s brand concept. Strong colors can also catch consumers’ attention in the first place.*
