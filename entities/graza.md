# Graza

> 来源：DIELINE — Graza Olive Oil Embraces Sustainability With New Refill Option

## 概述

Graza 的包装设计案例，收录于 DIELINE 文章。


## 关键特点

- Now, announced today, the brand is introducing a refill option featuring aluminum cans alongside its traditional plastic squeeze bottle format. This new approach, with design from Gander, addresses the need for a more sustainable option and helps ensure the oil’s freshness and quality.
