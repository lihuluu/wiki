# Walmart Great Value

> 来源：DIELINE — Walmart Streamlines Private Label Brand Great Value With A Sleek Redesign Across 10000 Products

## 概述

Walmart Great Value 的包装设计案例，收录于 DIELINE 文章。


## 关键特点

- The bigger the brand, the bigger the redesign—and it doesn’t get much bigger than Walmart.  
品牌越大，改版规模就越大——而沃尔玛的规模堪称业界之最。
- The behemoth retailer has just unveiled a massive overhaul of its flagship private label brand, Great Value, including the redesign of nearly 10,000 food and household items. That’s a lot of.ai files.  
这家零售巨头刚刚对其旗舰自有品牌 Great Value 进行了大规模改造，包括重新设计近万种食品和家居用品。这涉及大量.ai 文件。
- And a meaningful upgrade they’ve made, scrubbing Great Value’s sea of dated, disjointed designs and upgrading to a clear and cohesive visual language suited for the modern day shopper.  
他们确实实现了质的飞跃：彻底摒弃了"超值"品牌陈旧割裂的视觉体系，升级为清晰连贯的视觉语言，以契合当代消费者的审美需求。
